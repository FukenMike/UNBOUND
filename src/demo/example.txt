CHAPTER ONE

It was a bright cold day in April, and the clocks were striking thirteen. The street was oddly quiet.

Winston slipped quickly through the glass doors of Victory Mansions, though not quickly enough to prevent a swirl of gritty dust from entering along with him.

***

He moved towards the stairs. It was no use trying the lift.

CHAPTER TWO

As he put his hand to the doorknob Winston saw that he had left the diary open on the table.

---
